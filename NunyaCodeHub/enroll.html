Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

When do classes start?

Classes begin on July 7, 2025. All courses run on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM GMT to accommodate working professionals.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required! Our courses are designed for beginners, and we provide all the foundational knowledge you need to succeed.

What equipment do I need?

You'll need a computer with internet access. We'll provide detailed system requirements and software installation guides before the course starts.

Are there payment plans available?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans to make our courses accessible. Contact us at info@nunyacodehub.com for payment options.

Will I get job placement assistance?

Absolutely! We provide career guidance, resume review, interview preparation, and connect you with our network of hiring partners.
