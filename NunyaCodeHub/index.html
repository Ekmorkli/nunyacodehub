About NunyaCode Hub

"Nunya" comes from the Ewe word meaning "Knowledge" or "Skill" - the foundation of everything we do. Founded in 2025, NunyaCode Hub was created to bridge Africa's tech skills gap through AI-enhanced learning experiences.

We believe that every African deserves access to world-class tech education. Our programs are designed specifically for the African context, preparing students for the opportunities in our rapidly growing digital economy.
